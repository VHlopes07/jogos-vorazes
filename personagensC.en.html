CHARACTERS I

Gale Hawthorne

Katniss Everdeen, ao adentrar o último capítulo da trilogia Jogos Vorazes, confronta-se com Gale Hawthorne, seu amigo de longa data. A relação entre Katniss e Gale, agora enredada em questões políticas e morais, reflete a complexidade das alianças e a divisão que a revolução traz. Gale, com sua determinação e habilidade em liderança, desempenha um papel crucial na resistência, mas a sombra de diferenças fundamentais coloca à prova a amizade outrora inabalável.

Alma Coin

Katniss's encounter with Alma Coin, the leader of the rebellion, reveals the complexities and challenges of revolutionary authority. Coin's strategic skill is undeniable, but her quest for power and control creates tensions with Katniss. The dynamics between the two characters highlights the political nuances of the revolution, questioning the nature of leadership in times of conflict.

Boggs and Cressida

Katniss' introduction to Boggs and Cressida, allies in the mission to the heart of the Capitol, highlights the diversity of skills in the resistance. Boggs, with his military experience and loyalty, emerges as a credible presence, while Cressida, the rebel filmmaker, captures the fight in striking images. The coming together of these unlikely allies symbolizes the essential collaboration in the pursuit of freedom and the exposure of hidden truth.
